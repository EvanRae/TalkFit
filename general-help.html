General Help

Anxiety

Anxiety can be crippling on a day to day basis. Therapy can help reduce the level of threat and fear by enabling you to cope a little bit better.

Stress

Stress can be unquantifiable, and symptoms may have no apparent origin. Therapy may help identify the cause possibly enabling you to think and react in a different way alleviating some or all of the symptoms.

Young People

At a time when your brain is developing at speed and social expectations are high, a non-judgmental, confidential space to talk can help.
