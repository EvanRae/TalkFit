We are here to help

Here at TalkFit we are dedicated to offering you as much support as you need from ages 11 and up in a confidential, non-judgmental space. We offer both specialised help as well as general support. For more information on our services and how to book please see “our services” page


Specialized Help

Our specialized help is available for those who are in immidiate need of help. This includes people suffering with issues such as:

Depression

Grief and loss

Substance abuse

If any of these issues apply to you please see the “specialized help” page

General Help

We also offer help to people stuggling with aspects of everyday life and need help seeing their problems from a different point of view. If you struggle with anxiety, stress, are a young person who cant seem to keep up with society’s demands, or anything that you need to talk about please see the “General Help” page and drop us an email or book an apointment.
